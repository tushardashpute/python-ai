API controls:
a. temperature  - controls randomness
0.0 -> deterministic
0.8 -> Creative
1.0 -> very creative 

b. max_tokens - its bascically your max budget . limits your ouput 

c. top_p 
controls the probablity sampling. 

These are control levers, not just parameters.


top_p 
increases the most likely words vs most probablistic words 

AI is very .....
powerful -> 40% 
useful -> 30%
important -> 20%
wierd -> 5%
dumb -> 1%

if my top_p = 1.0 
model can pick anythihng from the above list.even "dumb" the one with 1% of even if 0.1% is there it can pick it up.
top_p=0.7 
now we only top words until cumilative = 70% 
powerful -> 40% 
useful -> 30%

Stop after (40+30%)

in practice we usually use or tune one of them - temperature or top_p and NOT both aggressively 
temperature - how the random choice is 
top_p - how many choices to chose from.

top_p is the preferred choice because it avoids extremely unlikely tokens. 

Core Concept.
LLMs are stateless - but conversations feel stateful.

stateless
